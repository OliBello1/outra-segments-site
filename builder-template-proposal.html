Every home has an ideal buyer

Introducing Outra’s Propensity to Buy modelling

Only c.4–7% of UK households move or buy each year. For a specific home, the true buyer pool can fall below 1%. Outra matches each property and ad to the households most likely to move, afford and want it.

70% reduction in cost per qualified lead
43% higher click-to-conversion

Based on Outra testing across 10+ homebuilders against broad and property-portal audiences.

Best results when creative and messaging is matched to the specific house type and site audiences.

Billions of signals including
Type of home
Willingness to move
Deposit affordability
Mortgage affordability
Catchment radius

Our Propensity to Buy model helps housebuilders stop wasting budget on broad audiences and focus on the households most likely to convert. It combines two critical dimensions, can they buy and will they buy.

Using affordability modelling, life-stage signals, property fit, location dynamics and real-world moving behaviour to identify buyers with both the financial capacity and genuine intent to purchase your development.

Built and validated on real transaction and move data, the model ranks households by likelihood to buy within the next 12 months, giving you a far sharper, more commercially valuable audience for every campaign.
